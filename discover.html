Why I Chose UMD

Choosing the University of Minnesota Duluth was one of the best decisions I've made. The natural surroundings of Duluth, with its proximity to Lake Superior and countless hiking trails, provide the perfect environment for someone who loves the outdoors as much as I do. Beyond the stunning scenery, the Labovitz School of Business and Economics offers an exceptional accounting program with excellent job placement rates, giving me confidence in my future career prospects.

Additionally, being close to home allows me to maintain strong connections with my family while pursuing my education and personal growth. UMD truly offers the perfect balance of academic excellence, natural beauty, and personal convenience.
